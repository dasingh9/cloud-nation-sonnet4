Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and process

How long does it take to develop a website?

The timeline depends on the complexity and requirements. A simple website typically takes 1-4 weeks, while complex applications can take 2-6 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our consultation.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive support and maintenance packages to keep your software updated, secure, and running smoothly. We're here for you even after launch.

What technologies do you specialize in?

We work with modern technologies including React, Next.js, Node.js, .NET, mobile development frameworks, cloud platforms like AWS and Azure, and various databases.

Can you work with our existing systems?

Absolutely! We specialize in system integration and can work with your existing infrastructure to enhance functionality or create new connections between systems.

How do you handle project communication?

We believe in transparent communication. You'll have regular updates, access to project management tools, and direct contact with our development team throughout the process.

What happens if I need changes after launch?

We're flexible! Minor adjustments are often included, and for larger changes, we can discuss additional development work. Our goal is your complete satisfaction with the final product.
